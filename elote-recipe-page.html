Mexican Elote Recipe

Home

Ingredients

  • 4 ears of corn
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup crumbled cotija cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • Chopped fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat a grill or stovetop grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Remove the husks and silk from the corn.
  3. Grill the corn, turning occasionally, until it becomes charred and tender, for about 10-12 minutes.
  4. In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, sour cream, cotija cheese, chili powder, paprika, garlic powder, salt, black pepper, and lime juice.
  5. Brush the grilled corn with the mayonnaise mixture, or roll the corn in the mixture to coat it evenly.
  6. Garnish with chopped cilantro.
  7. Serve the Mexican Elote immediately, optionally sprinkled with additional cotija cheese and chili powder if desired.
