Churros Recipe

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar (for coating)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon (for coating)

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, combine water, white sugar, salt, and vegetable oil. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat.
  2. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the flour until the mixture forms a ball of dough.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep frying pan or pot to 375°F (190°C).
  4. Transfer the churro dough to a piping bag fitted with a large star tip.
  5. Pipe 4-6 inch long strips of dough into the hot oil, cutting them with scissors or a knife.
  6. Fry the churros until they become golden brown and crispy, then remove them from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  7. In a shallow dish, combine granulated sugar and ground cinnamon.
  8. Roll the warm churros in the sugar-cinnamon mixture until coated.
  9. Serve the churros warm with your favorite dipping sauce such as chocolate sauce or caramel sauce.
