Carne Asada Tacos Recipe

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 pound flank steak
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine lime juice, soy sauce, minced garlic, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the flank steak in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over it. Ensure the steak is well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for better flavor.
  3. Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Remove the steak from the marinade and discard any excess marinade.
  5. Grill the steak for about 5-7 minutes per side, or until desired doneness. Remove from heat and let it rest for a few minutes.
  6. Thinly slice the steak against the grain.
  7. Warm up corn tortillas on the grill or stovetop.
  8. Assemble the tacos by placing a few slices of the grilled steak on each tortilla. Serve with your favorite toppings such as chopped onions, cilantro, salsa, guacamole, and lime wedges.
